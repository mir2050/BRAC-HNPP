FAQ

What are Teacher Accounts?

"Teacher Accounts" allows you to have multiple teachers under your account to teach and collaborate online. You can restrict teacher access using "read-only" and "full access" permissions to manage your courses, tests, contents, and live classes (virtual classroom).

How is edutube different?

E-Learning Platform is tailored for instructors to deliver any type of training online. It comes integrated with 4 core platforms in one unified solution - courses platform, online testing platform, virtual classroom, and content management system.

Do you offer complete branding?

Unlike Enterprise LMS product, E-Learning Platform does not offer custom branding such as your own domain, theme, logo and changing the platform identity to look like your own product. However, we do support uploading your own logo and changing the theme for the Virtual Classroom application.

Can I upgrade, downgrade or cancel at any time?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your account at any time. If you cancel your account, your portal will remain active until the end of your billing period.

What about security?

Data security is of utmost importance to us - all our traffic is done over SSL, the web standard for secure data transmission, and files are stored with top-grade secured infrastructure. Furthermore, we protect your audio and video files using signed URLs which are time based tokens that are only valid for a limited period of time.

Is there any limitation on the storage amount?

There is no storage limitation. The Free plan has a 100MB per uploaded file limit and paid plans have a 600MB per upload limit, but there is no cap on the total storage you may use.
